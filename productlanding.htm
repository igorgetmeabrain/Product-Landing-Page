About

Welcome to my gallery.
I am a professional violist, violin teacher, mum of three and artist.
I grew up in Cumbria, in a musical family. My parents performed regularly on the folk music scene, and my dad, Michael Johnson, was one of the most respected violin-makers in the country.
I trained as a violist at the Royal Northern College of Music, before embarking on a career as a freelance musician and teacher.
I began to devote a lot of time to painting in 2019, and my works quickly gained a following.
I paint and draw in a variety of mediums, including acrylics, watercolours and charcoal, and regularly sell my works throughout the UK and abroad.
